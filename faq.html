FAQ's

General Questions


What is Supercell?

Supercell is a Finnish mobile game developer known for creating highly engaging and addictive games.



What kind of games does Supercell develop?

Supercell specializes in creating free-to-play mobile games that are easy to pick up but hard to put down.



Where is Supercell headquartered?

Supercell is headquartered in Helsinki, Finland.

Games Realted Questions


Can the games be played on Laptop/PC ?

You can play the games on Laptop/PC using an emulator such as Bluestacks.



How do I make in-app purchases ?

You can make in-app purchases using any UPI ID or using Google Play Services.



I'm having trouble with in-game Resources. What should I do?

Contact Supercell support immediately with detailed information about the issue, including screenshots if possible.

Technical Issues


I'm having trouble downloading a game

Check internet, free storage. If issues persist, try later or contact app store support.



My game is crashing

Close other apps, update game and device. Clear cache, reinstall if needed, contact support.



I'm experiencing lag or slow performance

Close apps, check internet, lower graphics. Restart device, update software, free storage, contact support.

Account Issues


How can I secure my account ?

Enable two-factor authentication, use a strong password, and be cautious of phishing attempts. Contact Supercell support if your account has been compromised.



Can I restore my progress once I delete the game and restore it ?

If your game is linked to a Supercell ID, you can restore your progress. If not, contact Supercell support immediately.


Can I change the In-game Name

Check the specific rules for name changes. If you encounter issues, contact Supercell support.
