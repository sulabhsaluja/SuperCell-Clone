Clash of Clans

Troops

Super Balloon

4.2/5

Balloons are aerial units in Clash of Clans that excel at taking down defensive buildings. They have high hit points and decent damage, making them effective against air defenses and other structures.

Giant

4/5

Giants are the brawny, slow-moving behemoths of Clash of Clans. Their primary role is to absorb damage, acting as a shield for your other troops. With their towering stature and impressive health, they can withstand a beating from most defenses.

Wizard

4.7/5

Wizards are the spell-slinging sorcerers of Clash of Clans. These nimble magic-users pack a potent punch with their explosive fireballs. Their ability to target multiple enemies with each shot makes them a deadly force on the battlefield.

Witch

4.3/5

Witches are dark magic-wielding units in Clash of Clans. They are a unique troop that combines direct damage with the ability to summon skeletons. This makes them a versatile and powerful addition to any army.

Electro Dragon

4.7/5

Electro Dragons are formidable aerial units in Clash of Clans, known for their devastating chain lightning attacks. These powerful creatures can strike multiple targets with each strike, making them incredibly effective against clusters of buildings and troops.

P.E.K.K.A

4.8/5

The P.E.K.K.A. is a colossal, heavily armored unit in Clash of Clans. Renowned for its immense health and destructive power, it's a force to be reckoned with on the battlefield. This towering warrior is capable of obliterating buildings with each swing of its massive sword.
